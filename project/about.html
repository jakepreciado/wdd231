Jake Preciado: Game Developer & Founder of Arkuna Interactive

From a very young age, I was fascinated by the worlds that games could create. I would lose myself in these immersive environments, constantly amazed at how a simple idea could become a vast, dynamic universe. It wasn’t just about playing the games – it was about understanding how they worked, how they came to life, and how they made me feel. I was always drawn to the idea of imagination taking shape and becoming something tangible. As I grew up, this fascination evolved into a deep desire to create my own worlds. I realized that storytelling didn’t have to be confined to books or movies – it could live and breathe in the interactive space of games. I wanted to craft experiences where players didn’t just observe a story, but became a part of it. That’s when I decided to pursue game development. The name “Arkuna” is a reflection of my vision. “Ark” is derived from "Architect," representing my drive to build worlds. “Una” means “one,” symbolizing my path as a solo developer, determined to bring my unique ideas to life on my own terms. Arkuna Interactive is the beginning of my journey—a studio where I can build not just games, but experiences that allow players to immerse themselves in worlds of my creation. With Arkuna Interactive, I’m dedicated to bringing stories to life through interactive games that challenge, inspire, and connect players. It’s not just about code and mechanics—it’s about making players feel like they’ve stepped into something that’s truly their own. I’m excited to continue this journey and see where it takes me.
